What Counts Here

Official Monday miles come from documented Morning Run Club runs. For each run, the tracker uses the distance and the number of runners who were there.

Official Monday Miles = Distance × Runners Present

Some older runs were posted before we started tracking distance and runners, so this page only counts runs that actually have that data.

Total member mileage is different. It includes eligible Strava running activities from connected club members and is tracked separately from official Monday miles.

Connect Strava

Runners can connect their Strava accounts so eligible runs can count toward the club’s total member mileage. Official Monday miles and total member miles are tracked separately.

Connect Strava Account

Connected runners’ public Strava running activities can be added to the club mileage total during syncs.

Strava Mileage Rules

Total member miles are separate from official Monday miles. Strava mileage includes eligible running activities from connected club members.

  • Only running activities count.
  • Activities must be visible to Strava in order to sync.
  • Runs are counted once and duplicates are ignored.
  • Official Monday miles are still tracked separately from total member miles.
