Ronald McDonald House Charities

Morning Run Club supports Ronald McDonald House Charities through our charity race. We chose this cause because it helps families stay close to their children during difficult medical situations. We wanted our running to support something bigger than just the club.

Why We Support Them

The goal of Morning Run Club is not just to run. It is to use running to build discipline, faith, community, and service. Supporting Ronald McDonald House Charities gives the club a real reason to bring people together.

How the Club Has Helped

So far, Morning Run Club has raised $2,730 through its charity race. The race brought together runners, organizers, and supporters around one purpose: using a simple running event to help families in the community.

Looking Ahead

The plan is to keep supporting Ronald McDonald House Charities through future charity race efforts. The club is still growing, but the goal is to keep making the runs and races more organized, more meaningful, and more helpful each year.
