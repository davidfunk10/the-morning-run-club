How the Club Has Grown

Morning Run Club started as a simple idea: meet before school, share a Bible verse, and run together. Since then, the club has grown through weekly runs, a charity race, and a small group of students committed to showing up consistently.

Charity Race Impact

Morning Run Club hosted a charity race that raised $2,740 and had 17 finishers. The race gave students and community members a way to use running to support a real cause.

Running Community Impact

The club meets for selected Monday morning runs before school. Not every run is posted on the blog, but the goal has stayed the same: start the week with discipline, movement, and community.

Faith and Leadership Impact

Before runs, members share a Bible verse to connect the morning with faith and purpose. The club is still small, but the focus is on consistency, leadership, and building something that can keep growing over time.
